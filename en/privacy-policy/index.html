Privacy Policy

CV para Todos is built on a local-first model. We do not store personal data on servers and we do not have access to content created inside the platform.

Where data is stored

All CV data is stored exclusively in your browser (LocalStorage / IndexedDB). It never leaves your device.

OAuth / autenticación

If you use external login providers, only temporary session tokens are used. We do not store credentials or passwords.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is only activated after explicit consent via the cookie banner. Data collected is aggregated and anonymous.

How to view your data in browser

You can view and delete locally stored data in your browser using these steps:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies → View site data.
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Cookies and Site Data → Manage data.
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies & site permissions → View cookies.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage website data.

User control

You have full control over your data. You can delete it at any time from your browser. There is no central database.
