Cookie Policy

At CV para Todos we only use cookies when strictly necessary for the site to function and for anonymous analytics. Non-essential cookies such as Google Analytics require your explicit consent through the cookie banner.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored in your browser that enable basic website functionality or help analyze usage.

Types of cookies used

  • Google Analytics: only activated with your consent. Used for anonymous analytics.
  • Local storage: CV data remains on your device and is not sent to servers.
  • OAuth: temporary session tokens for authentication.

Consent

Non-essential cookies are only activated after your explicit consent via the banner. You can withdraw or modify consent at any time.
